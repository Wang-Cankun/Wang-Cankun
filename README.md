## What I Do

- **AI Agent Systems** - RAG pipelines, knowledge graphs, LLM integration for biomedical research workflows
- **Cloud Infrastructure** - Designing and managing HIPAA-compliant research computing platforms (AWS, Azure)
- **Multi-omics Analysis** - Single-cell, spatial transcriptomics, and multi-omics data for cancer immunotherapy and neurodegenerative diseases
- **Full-stack Development** - Research web applications, databases, and knowledgebases (Vue.js, NestJS, TypeScript, MySQL)
